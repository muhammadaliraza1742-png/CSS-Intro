Why choose FocusFlow?

Smart Time Boxing

Divide your workday into manageable intervals separated by short breaks to keep your mind fresh.

Zero Distractions

A clean, minimalist interface that gets out of your way so you can focus entirely on your tasks.

Progress Insights

Track your daily focus streaks and see where your time goes with simple, beautiful charts.

How it works?




  1. Choose a high-priority task you need to accomplish.
  2. Set your FocusFlow timer for 25 minutes and work without interruption.
  3. Take a short 5-minute break to recharge before your next session.
